The premier car soccer game! Be dazzled by the 16 color palette with amazing 
futuristic 8 BIT TECHNOLOGY! Drive to victory in One (1) amazing field, boosting
past your inferior opponents! Can you score nine goals, unlock the secrets of 
the Soccer Car Elders, and become the greatest...MISSILE CHAMPION?

Windows Only.

Controls:
Space - Jump (Gameplay)  / Select (Title / Car Selection)
Z - Boost
X - Switch Cars
Up - Accelerate
Down - Reverse
Left / Right - Turn
Escape - Return to title / Exit game (Title)
